# Bias Patterns

Purpose:

- Record recurring bias patterns seen across TB studies so future critique becomes faster and sharper.

Common headings:

- selection bias
- immortal time bias
- time-varying confounding
- confounding by indication
- informative censoring
- missing outcome data
- outcome misclassification
- multiplicity and subgroup overreach

For each note:

- study context
- bias pattern
- how it shows up
- what damage it causes
- how to reduce it in future work
