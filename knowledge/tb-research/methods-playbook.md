# Methods Playbook

Purpose:

- Store reusable methodological lessons that should influence future research design.

Template:

- `YYYY-MM-DD | TOPIC | FACT | method lesson`
- `YYYY-MM-DD | TOPIC | INFERENCE | how this changes design choices`
- `YYYY-MM-DD | TOPIC | HYPOTHESIS | where this lesson may generalize`

Priority topics:

- target trial thinking
- causal inference
- pragmatic trial design
- missing data
- competing risks
- relapse definitions
- adherence measurement
- transportability

2026-04-08 | SURVEILLANCE | INFERENCE | "Empty but verified" is a success state for automated surveillance because disciplined non-inclusion protects downstream design decisions from low-signal noise.
2026-04-08 | SURVEILLANCE | HYPOTHESIS | Over time, a monitoring system that records null-result days cleanly will produce more trusted escalation when a truly decision-relevant TB study appears.
