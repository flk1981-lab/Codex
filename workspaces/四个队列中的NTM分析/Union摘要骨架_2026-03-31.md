# Union摘要骨架

基于 The Union World Conference on Lung Health 的 Scientific Research 摘要格式整理：

- `Background`
- `Design/Methods`
- `Results`
- `Conclusions`

参考提交要求：

- 摘要正文不超过 300 词
- 必须包含至少初步结果，不能写成“结果待补”
- 更适合把本研究定位为 `Scientific Research`

来源：

- https://conf2025.theunion.org/abstract-submission-guidelines/

## 推荐题目

优先题目：

Occurrence, species distribution and within-species genomic relatedness of nontuberculous mycobacteria emerging during anti-tuberculosis treatment in four prospective cohorts in Shenzhen, China

备选题目 1：

Nontuberculous mycobacteria emerging during anti-tuberculosis treatment: findings from four prospective tuberculosis cohorts in Shenzhen, China

备选题目 2：

Species distribution and genomic relatedness of nontuberculous mycobacteria isolated during anti-tuberculosis treatment in four prospective cohorts

## 建议摘要定位

这篇摘要最稳妥的主问题不是“NTM 是否传播”，而是：

1. 四个前瞻性 TB 队列中，抗结核治疗期间 NTM 的发生率和菌种分布如何；
2. 成功复苏并获得 WGS 的部分菌株中，是否存在同种 NTM 的高度遗传相关菌群。

因此，分子部分建议统一表述为：

- `within-species genomic relatedness`
- `within-species SNP distance analysis`
- `exploratory molecular epidemiological analysis`

不建议在摘要里直接写：

- `homology analysis`
- `clonal transmission`（除非同时有明确流行病学关联和稳健阈值支持）
- `outbreak`（目前样本结构不足以支撑）

## 可直接改写成英文摘要的骨架

### Background

Nontuberculous mycobacteria (NTM) may emerge during anti-tuberculosis treatment and complicate clinical assessment, particularly in settings with high burdens of both tuberculosis (TB) and NTM. However, data from prospective TB treatment cohorts are limited. We assessed the occurrence and species distribution of NTM isolated during anti-TB treatment across four prospective cohorts in Shenzhen, China, and explored within-species genomic relatedness among successfully revived isolates.

### Design/Methods

We conducted an observational analysis nested within four prospective TB treatment cohorts, including drug-susceptible TB, TB with diabetes, and two multidrug-resistant TB cohorts. NTM episodes detected during anti-TB treatment were identified through routine mycobacterial culture and species identification. We summarised NTM occurrence and species distribution across cohorts. Successfully revived isolates were selected for whole-genome sequencing (WGS). Genomic analysis was planned and interpreted within species rather than across all NTM species combined. Mixed MTB-NTM isolates were excluded from the primary genomic relatedness analysis or analysed separately after quality assessment. For species with sufficient numbers of isolates, we estimated pairwise SNP distances and reconstructed phylogenetic relationships; species represented by only a few isolates were described descriptively.

### Results

Among 821 participants across four prospective TB cohorts, 55 NTM isolates were identified during anti-TB treatment, corresponding to an overall occurrence of 6.7%. NTM occurrence was 5.5% (13/235) in the drug-susceptible TB cohort, 2.8% (5/181) in the TB-diabetes cohort, 9.8% (12/122) in the MDR-Chin cohort, and 8.8% (25/283) in the SEAL-MDR cohort. Of the 55 isolates, 48 were successfully revived for sequencing and seven failed revival. The predominant species was Mycobacterium abscessus complex (25/48 sequenced isolates), followed by M. fortuitum (5/48), M. intracellulare (3/48) and M. gordonae (3/48); five isolates had mixed MTB-NTM identification. Because most non-abscessus species were represented by small numbers, genomic relatedness analysis was expected to be interpretable mainly for the dominant species and exploratory for the remainder.

### Conclusions

NTM isolation during anti-TB treatment was not uncommon in these four prospective TB cohorts, particularly in multidrug-resistant TB cohorts, and the species distribution was heterogeneous. For WGS-based molecular epidemiology, a species-specific analytic framework is essential. In this dataset, within-species genomic relatedness analysis is most feasible for the dominant species, while cross-species pooling would be methodologically inappropriate and may overinterpret transmission. Final WGS results will help clarify whether closely related strain clusters exist and inform the distinction between sporadic isolation and possible shared sources or transmission.

## 中文写作要点

### 题目

题目尽量保留三个信息块：

- `anti-tuberculosis treatment期间`
- `four prospective cohorts`
- `species distribution + genomic relatedness`

### 方法段

方法段要强调三件事：

1. 四个前瞻性 TB 队列的嵌套观察性分析；
2. WGS 只对成功复苏菌株开展；
3. 遗传相关性分析按物种分别进行，而不是把所有 NTM 合并。

### 结果段

Union 摘要最看重“有数字的结果”，所以结果段优先顺序建议是：

1. 总发生率和各队列发生率；
2. 55 株总体、48 株成功复苏、7 株失败；
3. 成功送检菌株的主要物种分布；
4. 最后一句再交代分子分析的解释边界。

### “同源性分析”严谨表达

建议用下面这套口径：

- 中文：`种内遗传相关性分析`、`基于WGS的种内SNP距离分析`、`探索性分子流行病学分析`
- 英文：`within-species genomic relatedness analysis`、`within-species SNP distance analysis`

并明确说明：

- 不同 NTM 物种不能直接放在一起做统一 SNP 距离解释；
- `MTB+NTM` 混合标注菌株不应直接进入主要种内分析；
- 对单株或仅 2-3 株的物种，最多做描述性比较，不宜下传播结论；
- 即使发现遗传距离很近，也应写成 `closely related isolates` 或 `possible clusters`，避免直接写 `transmission`。

## 下一版建议

等 WGS 结果出来后，优先补这几类信息进入摘要：

1. 实际纳入主分析的物种和样本数；
2. 质控后保留样本数；
3. 主要物种的 SNP 距离范围和中位数；
4. 是否存在若干高度接近的 isolate pairs or clusters；
5. 这些 cluster 是否有时间或空间流行病学关联。
