# TB Formal Learning Report (2026-03-30)

## English Version

### 1. 3 to 5 Most Worthwhile Items Today

#### 1) Long-term mortality after TB diagnosis and treatment

- FACT: A Nature Medicine study published online on 2026-03-19 reported that mortality remained elevated after treatment for drug-susceptible pulmonary TB in South Africa, with risk highest early but still persisting long term.
- FACT: This was a population-based matched cohort using linked routine data rather than a randomized trial.
- INFERENCE: The study argues that "treatment success" should not be treated as the last clinically meaningful endpoint.
- HYPOTHESIS: Future TB-DM and post-TB protocols may need explicit post-treatment survival and cardiopulmonary follow-up instead of stopping at microbiological cure.
- Source: https://www.nature.com/articles/s41591-026-04294-w

#### 2) Inpatient same-day 1HP in advanced HIV plus cryptococcal meningitis

- FACT: A Lancet HIV strategy trial in Uganda showed that inpatient initiation of 1HP preventive therapy was non-inferior to deferred outpatient initiation in adults with advanced HIV disease and cryptococcal meningitis, with better completion.
- FACT: It was an open-label, randomized, non-inferiority trial with a 15% margin.
- INFERENCE: This is a strong example of a delivery-timing trial answering a real implementation bottleneck without changing the drug regimen itself.
- HYPOTHESIS: Similar "when to start" trials may be highly valuable in TB-DM and other high-risk TB care pathways.
- Source: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41662846/

#### 3) Pulmonary rehabilitation after pulmonary TB treatment

- FACT: A Chest randomized trial in Uganda found that pulmonary rehabilitation improved exercise capacity at 6 weeks in adults treated for pulmonary TB.
- FACT: The trial used a patient-centered functional endpoint rather than a microbiological endpoint.
- INFERENCE: Post-TB care is becoming a legitimate intervention field, not merely long-term observation.
- HYPOTHESIS: TB programs that only track recurrence may miss a large share of clinically meaningful post-treatment morbidity.
- Source: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41796938/

#### 4) WHO started collecting evidence for the next TB screening policy update

- FACT: On 2026-03-17, WHO issued a public call for data to inform updates on tools for screening for TB disease.
- FACT: The call explicitly mentioned symptom screening, chest X-ray with or without CAD, CRP in people living with HIV, and low-complexity automated NAATs, while inviting evidence on newer tools and pediatric use cases.
- INFERENCE: Screening guidance may move before treatment guidance does, so diagnostic and implementation studies should align now with the questions WHO is preparing to revisit.
- HYPOTHESIS: Studies not designed around forthcoming WHO screening PICO questions may be less influential even if technically strong.
- Source: https://www.who.int/news-room/articles-detail/public-call-for-data-to-inform-who-policy-updates-on-tools-for-screening-for-tb-disease

### 2. Mentor-Style Deep Dive

#### Focus paper: long-term mortality after TB diagnosis and treatment

- FACT: The paper asked whether treated TB patients return to baseline mortality risk, not merely whether treatment achieves microbiological cure.
- FACT: Its strength lies in real-world scale and downstream relevance rather than causal purity.
- FACT: The core signal was persistent excess mortality after treatment completion.
- INFERENCE: Many TB studies still behave as if the clinically meaningful story ends at treatment completion; this paper challenges that assumption.
- HYPOTHESIS: A high-value next step would be a post-TB prospective cohort with predefined cardiopulmonary, functional, and mortality outcomes.

### 3. New Methods Lessons From This Run

- FACT: Delivery-strategy trials can be as decision-relevant as regimen-comparison trials when the practical barrier is uptake or timing.
- FACT: Post-TB intervention trials can justify functional recovery endpoints if the clinical question is recovery rather than sterilization.
- FACT: WHO policy-scoping calls are not yet guideline changes, but they are early signals about which study designs may become more policy-relevant soon.
- INFERENCE: Our TB learning should not be trapped inside regimen efficacy alone; screening, implementation timing, and post-TB outcomes now matter equally.
- HYPOTHESIS: The most fundable future TB studies may increasingly be hybrid designs connecting microbiology, function, and implementation.

### 4. What Matters Most For Our Study Design Thinking

- FACT: A protocol can be internally polished and still be strategically weak if it ignores where TB evidence and policy are moving.
- FACT: This run reinforces three design priorities: longer post-treatment thinking, implementation-timing questions, and patient-centered recovery outcomes.
- INFERENCE: For TDSC-like work, the next leap is not more protocol text but better linkage to external evidence on who benefits, who remains at risk, and which outcomes matter after treatment ends.
- HYPOTHESIS: If future TB-DM studies use only microbiological endpoints and no post-treatment functional or survival lens, they may increasingly look incomplete.

### 5. The One Thing You Should Study Next

- Recommendation: make a one-page gap map titled "What outcomes matter after TB treatment ends?"
- FACT: This run produced direct support for three domains: mortality, functional recovery, and implementation timing.
- INFERENCE: That gap map will sharpen which future questions are genuinely clinically important versus only operationally convenient.
- HYPOTHESIS: Once that gap map is built, our next study ideas will likely become more mature and less protocol-centered.

## Chinese Version

### 1. 今日最值得看的3到5条

#### 1）TB 诊断和治疗后的长期死亡风险

- FACT：一篇于 `2026-03-19` 在线发表在 Nature Medicine 的研究显示，在南非药敏肺结核人群中，治疗完成后死亡风险仍持续高于匹配对照，且这种风险不只集中在短期。
- FACT：这是一项基于常规数据链接的人群匹配队列研究，而不是随机试验。
- INFERENCE：这篇文章提示我们，不能把“治疗成功”当作最后一个有临床意义的终点。
- HYPOTHESIS：今后的 TB-DM 和 post-TB 研究，可能需要把治疗后生存和心肺后遗症随访正式写进方案，而不只是盯住微生物学治愈。
- 来源：https://www.nature.com/articles/s41591-026-04294-w

#### 2）AHD 合并隐球脑膜炎人群住院内当天启动 1HP

- FACT：一项来自乌干达的 Lancet HIV 策略试验显示，在晚期 HIV 合并隐球脑膜炎患者中，住院期间启动 1HP 相比延后到门诊启动并不更差，而且完成率更好。
- FACT：这是一项开放标签、随机、非劣效设计试验，非劣效界值为 `15%`。
- INFERENCE：它很值得学习，因为它回答的是“什么时候启动更合理”这个真实实施问题，而不是机械地比较新旧药物方案。
- HYPOTHESIS：类似的“启动时机”试验，在 TB-DM 和其他高风险 TB 管理场景中也可能很有价值。
- 来源：https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41662846/

#### 3）肺结核治疗后的肺康复

- FACT：一项发表在 Chest 的随机试验发现，在乌干达完成肺结核治疗后的成人中，肺康复可以改善 6 周时的运动耐量。
- FACT：这项研究的主要终点是以患者功能恢复为中心的指标，而不是微生物学指标。
- INFERENCE：这说明 post-TB care 已经不只是“长期观察”，而正在成为真正可以做干预研究的领域。
- HYPOTHESIS：如果项目只追踪复发，而不看功能恢复与生活质量，可能会漏掉大量真正重要的治疗后负担。
- 来源：https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41796938/

#### 4）WHO 已开始为下一轮 TB 筛查政策更新征集证据

- FACT：WHO 于 `2026-03-17` 发布公告，公开征集用于更新 TB 疾病筛查工具政策的证据。
- FACT：公告明确提到症状筛查、胸片及 CAD、HIV 人群 CRP、低复杂度自动化 NAAT，也欢迎新工具和儿科场景数据。
- INFERENCE：这提示筛查政策的更新可能先于治疗政策，因此诊断和实施研究最好尽早按 WHO 即将重审的问题来设计。
- HYPOTHESIS：如果研究没有围绕 WHO 即将采用的 PICO 问题组织，即使技术上不错，政策影响力也可能有限。
- 来源：https://www.who.int/news-room/articles-detail/public-call-for-data-to-inform-who-policy-updates-on-tools-for-screening-for-tb-disease

### 2. 重点论文导师拆解

#### 重点论文：TB 诊断和治疗后的长期死亡风险

- FACT：这篇文章真正回答的问题不是“TB 是否被治愈”，而是“治愈后的患者是否真正回到了基线风险”。
- FACT：它的优势在于真实世界规模和长期结局价值，而不是随机试验式的因果纯度。
- FACT：核心结论是治疗完成后仍存在持续的超额死亡风险。
- INFERENCE：很多 TB 研究默认“疗程结束”就是故事终点，但这篇文章说明这个终点太短了。
- HYPOTHESIS：下一步最有价值的研究，可能是前瞻性 post-TB 队列，把心肺功能、生活质量和死亡都预先定义为关键结局。

### 3. 本次新增的方法学启发

- FACT：当真正的瓶颈是启动时机或落地执行时，实施策略试验和药物比较试验同样重要。
- FACT：如果临床问题是“恢复得怎样”，post-TB 干预研究完全可以把功能恢复作为主要终点。
- FACT：WHO 的政策征数公告虽然不是正式指南更新，但它已经在提示今后什么类型的研究更容易进入政策讨论。
- INFERENCE：我们对 TB 的学习不能只停留在“方案是否有效”，还要同时关注筛查、实施路径和治疗后结局。
- HYPOTHESIS：未来更有资助吸引力的 TB 研究，可能是把微生物学、功能恢复和实施科学放在一起的混合设计。

### 4. 对我们研究设计最有价值的提醒

- FACT：一个 protocol 即使内部写得很工整，如果脱离外部证据和政策走向，战略上仍然可能偏弱。
- FACT：这次学习最值得记住的三个方向是：更长的治疗后视角、启动时机类研究、以患者恢复为中心的结局。
- INFERENCE：对 TDSC 这类研究来说，下一步真正的提升不在于写更多文本，而在于把项目内设计和外部证据更紧密地连起来。
- HYPOTHESIS：如果今后的 TB-DM 研究仍只看微生物学终点，而不看治疗后功能和生存，它们会越来越显得不完整。

### 5. 你今天最值得补的一件事

- 建议：做一页 gap map，标题就叫“TB 治疗结束后，真正重要的结局是什么？”
- FACT：这次运行直接支持了 3 个领域：死亡风险、功能恢复、实施时机。
- INFERENCE：这张 gap map 会帮助我们区分哪些问题是真正重要，哪些只是操作上方便。
- HYPOTHESIS：一旦这张图做出来，后续研究想法会明显更成熟，也不会再过度停留在 protocol 层面。
