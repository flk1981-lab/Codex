# BDLM严格终点结果段落草稿

## 中文版

基于 Union 摘要最小数据集，共录入 122 例受试者，去重后为 121 例。按研究方案保留 `BDL` 或 `BDLM` 治疗策略后，共纳入 108 例进入本次描述性分析，其中 `BDL` 49 例，`BDLM` 59 例。根据预设的严格终点重建规则，主结局定义为停药后 6 个月时的结局状态，且将失访和退出从“真正失败/复发”中单独分离。总体上，停药后 6 个月真正失败/复发 2/108 例（1.9%），失访 17/108 例（15.7%），退出 8/108 例（7.4%），其他非方案性不良结局 1/108 例（0.9%），随访完成且无事件 56/108 例（51.9%）；另有 24/108 例（22.2%）截至 2026 年 3 月 31 日尚未达到该随访时间窗。分组描述性结果显示，`BDL` 组停药后 6 个月真正失败/复发 2/49 例（4.1%），`BDLM` 组未观察到真正失败/复发事件；但由于两组并非随机分配，相关组间结果仅作描述，不作因果解释。

次要结局定义为停药后 12 个月时的严格结局状态。总体上，停药后 12 个月真正失败/复发 2/108 例（1.9%），失访 36/108 例（33.3%），退出 8/108 例（7.4%），其他非方案性不良结局 1/108 例（0.9%），随访完成且无事件 37/108 例（34.3%）；另有 24/108 例（22.2%）尚未达到 12 个月随访时间窗。`BDL` 组和 `BDLM` 组停药后 12 个月真正失败/复发分别为 2/49 例（4.1%）和 0/59 例（0.0%）。值得注意的是，12 个月时较高的“不良结局”负担主要由失访和退出驱动，而非真实失败/复发事件；其中被归类为停药后 12 个月失访的患者目前仍在继续尝试联络，因此这些病例应理解为结局待进一步澄清，而不能直接视为已经证实的不良治疗结局。

如需更强调培养学结果，可补充描述：在可评估者中，治疗 2 个月培养阴转率为 90/101（89.1%），停药时培养阴性率为 78/78（100.0%）。

## 英文版

A total of 122 participants were entered in the Union minimal dataset; after deduplication, 121 unique records remained. Restricting the analysis to protocol-consistent `BDL` or `BDLM` strategy groups yielded 108 participants, including 49 in the `BDL` group and 59 in the `BDLM` group. Using the prespecified strict endpoint reconstruction, the primary endpoint was defined as outcome status at 6 months after treatment completion, with loss to follow-up and withdrawal analyzed separately from true failure/relapse. Overall, true failure/relapse occurred in 2/108 participants (1.9%), loss to follow-up in 17/108 (15.7%), withdrawal in 8/108 (7.4%), other non-protocol unfavorable outcomes in 1/108 (0.9%), and completed follow-up without events in 56/108 (51.9%); an additional 24/108 participants (22.2%) had not yet reached the 6-month follow-up window as of March 31, 2026. Descriptively, true failure/relapse at 6 months was observed in 2/49 participants (4.1%) in the `BDL` group and in 0/59 participants (0.0%) in the `BDLM` group. Because treatment strategy was assigned according to drug-susceptibility results rather than randomization, between-group comparisons should be interpreted descriptively only.

The secondary endpoint was strict outcome status at 12 months after treatment completion. Overall, true failure/relapse was observed in 2/108 participants (1.9%), loss to follow-up in 36/108 (33.3%), withdrawal in 8/108 (7.4%), other non-protocol unfavorable outcomes in 1/108 (0.9%), and completed follow-up without events in 37/108 (34.3%); 24/108 participants (22.2%) had not yet reached the 12-month follow-up window. The proportions of true failure/relapse at 12 months were 2/49 (4.1%) in the `BDL` group and 0/59 (0.0%) in the `BDLM` group. Importantly, the apparently high burden of “unfavorable outcomes” at 12 months was driven primarily by loss to follow-up and withdrawal rather than confirmed failure/relapse. Participants classified as lost to follow-up at 12 months are still being actively contacted; therefore, these cases should be considered outcomes pending clarification rather than confirmed unfavorable treatment outcomes.

If microbiological response needs to be highlighted, an additional sentence may be used: among evaluable participants, the culture conversion rate at month 2 was 90/101 (89.1%), and the end-of-treatment culture negativity rate was 78/78 (100.0%).
